This web-app is a data visualizer about energy production, CO2 emissions and the effect it has in the natural habitats. Use the drop-down menus to create the graph with the stats you want to visualize.Keep in mind:

Line Charts: The total number of lines in the chart must not exceed 12, because then the graph will be unreadable.

Bar Charts: The total number of bars in the chart must not exceed 4, beacause then the graph will be messy.

Scatter Plot (for a given country): You pick exactly one country and 2 indicators. Aggregation is available.

Scatter Plot (for a given year): You pick just start year and 2 indicators. Aggregation is NOT available.

Data was retrieved from: The World Bank

The app was developed using: Angular 13.3.2 and Node.js 16.14.2
